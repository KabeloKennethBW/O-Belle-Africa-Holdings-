# IMS Control Model

This is an IMS design baseline, not evidence of certification.

## Core registers
- Document Master
- Legal & Obligations
- Enterprise Risk
- Product/SKU Master
- Supplier Register
- Claims Substantiation
- CAPA
- Complaints
- Traceability & Recall
- Decision Log
- Evidence Register
- Publication Register

## Product release logic
No SKU advances to pilot release without an approved specification, supplier/sample qualification, QC acceptance criteria, traceability design, controlled label copy and applicable regulatory/standards evidence.

## Claims release logic
Every proposed claim must identify claim type, required evidence, evidence held, approver and artwork permission. Default permission is NO.
