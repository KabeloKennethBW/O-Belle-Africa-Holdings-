# O’Belle Stage Gates

| Gate | Decision | Minimum control objective |
|---|---|---|
| G0 | Mobilised | Scope, registers and legal verification initiated |
| G1 | Research-ready | Founder dossier, governance and research instruments controlled |
| G2 | Market validated | Primary consumer evidence, market sizing and channel evidence |
| G3 | Commercial/technical feasible | Supplier quotations, product pathway, standards pathway and channel economics |
| G4 | Investment case supportable | Rebased finance, funding need, brand/IP decision and minimum IMS |
| G5 | Pilot-ready | Qualified product, QC, traceability, compliant artwork and pilot protocol |
| G6 | Pilot proven | Sell-through, repeat, quality, contribution and cash thresholds evaluated |
| G7 | Scale-authorised | Board approval, financing, contracts, controls and capacity plan |

Failure at a gate triggers evidence closure, pivot or hold rather than automatic progression.
