# O’Belle Full Solution Repository

Controlled project repository for O’Belle Africa Holdings (Pty) Ltd and its feminine-care venture.

## Current control status
O’Belle is treated as a validation/pre-launch venture. Generated dashboards, certifications, financial figures, product claims and historic operating metrics are not factual evidence unless promoted through the controlled evidence and approval process.

## Repository architecture
- `00_ADMIN/` — repository governance and control
- `01_CORPORATE_GOVERNANCE/` — corporate master data, governance and decisions
- `02_RESEARCH_EVIDENCE/` — research programme, evidence and market validation
- `03_STRATEGY_BUSINESS_PLAN/` — strategy, stage gates and business planning
- `04_FINANCE_FUNDING/` — assumptions, models, funding and scenario analysis
- `05_BRAND/` — O’Belle corporate, Dignity, Harmony and controlled brand assets
- `06_PRODUCT_RND_PACKAGING/` — PRDs, SKU master, product and packaging engineering
- `07_MANUFACTURING_FACILITY/` — make/buy analysis, process, machinery and facility planning
- `08_QUALITY_REGULATORY_IMS/` — IMS, quality, regulatory, CAPA, complaints and recall
- `09_SUPPLY_CHAIN_LOGISTICS/` — suppliers, RFQs, procurement, logistics and inventory
- `10_SALES_MARKETING_GTM/` — channel validation, pilots, sales and GTM
- `11_ESG_IMPACT/` — ESG methodology, sustainability and impact evidence
- `12_DIGITAL_SYSTEMS_DATA/` — IMS applications, schemas, integrations and dashboards
- `13_LEGAL_CONTRACTS_IP/` — contracts, IP, trademarks and legal controls
- `14_HR_ORGANISATION/` — organisation, roles, competence and training
- `15_DATA_ROOM_PUBLICATIONS/` — controlled audience publications and due diligence
- `90_ARCHIVE_SUPERSEDED/` — superseded/generated exploratory material
- `99_TEMPLATES_CONTROLS/` — reusable templates and schemas

## Stage-gate model
G0 Mobilised → G1 Research-ready → G2 Market validated → G3 Commercial/technical feasible → G4 Investment case supportable → G5 Pilot-ready → G6 Pilot proven → G7 Scale-authorised.

## Claims rule
Claims such as organic, biodegradable, hypoallergenic, dermatologically tested, Made in Botswana, BOBS/ISO compliance or certification are blocked from production artwork until the Claims Register contains appropriate evidence and approval.

## Confidentiality
This repository must not be used as a public data room. Confidential legal, financial, supplier, consumer-level and due-diligence evidence belongs in restricted storage. Public repository content should contain schemas, templates, non-confidential specifications and approved publications only.
